without worrying about negotiating fares or getting lost.

Features of Namma Yatri website

How it works

1. Firstly, register or login in Namma Yatri website

2. Enter your phone number for registeration process.

3. Allow location access to move ahead with the booking.

4. Enter pickup and drop-off points.

5. Next, you will be showed the estimated fare for the ride.

6. Tap on Request Driver to book a ride.
